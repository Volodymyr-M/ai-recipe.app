Questions home cooks ask us

If you're curious, you're in good company. Here are the answers people want before their first scan.

Does it work with low-light restaurant photos?

Yes. Love2Cook enhances dim or uneven lighting so you get reliable recognition whether you're dining out or eating in.

Can I adjust recipes for dietary needs?

Set your preferences once and we automatically surface recipes—and smart substitutions—that match your likes, dislikes, and allergies.

Will it help with meal planning?

Absolutely. Save scans, build weekly lineups, and export grocery lists or share recipes for group planning.

What phones are supported?

Love2Cook runs on current iOS and Android devices. iPhone users can enable voice control for hands-free cooking.
